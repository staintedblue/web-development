Travel to Hawaii

Hawaii is an island state in the Western United States, about 2,000 miles (3,200 km) from the U.S. mainland in the Pacific Ocean. It is the only U.S. state outside North America, the only one which is an archipelago, and the only one in the tropics.

Language:

  • English
  • Hawaiian

Volcanism

The island of Hawaiʻi is built from five separate shield volcanoes that erupted somewhat sequentially, one overlapping the other.
These are (from oldest to youngest):

  1. Kohala
  2. Mauna Kea
  3. Hualālai
  4. Mauna Loa
  5. Kīlauea
